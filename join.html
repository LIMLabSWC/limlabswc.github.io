We are hiring!

We have recently received a Consolidator Grant by the European Research Council (ERC) to investigate statistical learning in rats, mice and humans!

The award of €2 million over five years will enable us to continue to pursue our long-term research endeavours on statistical learning and its interaction with working memory. The funding will allow the recruitment of postdoctoral fellows as well as research technicians for up to five years.

Please get in touch if you are interested in joining our lab!
